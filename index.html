üè∞ How to Use the Castle Battle Scheduler

1

Add Alliances

Enter alliance names (like FTP, WUW, 686) and pick colors from the dropdown. Click "Add Alliance" for each one. Add "FFA" if you want Free-For-All slots.

2

Build Your Schedule

Click on any cell in the timeline grid to assign an alliance to that time slot. Each cell represents your selected time interval (15, 30, or 60 minutes).

3

Preview the Battle

Use the time slider under the Castle Map to scrub through the timeline. Watch the castle and turrets change colors to see who holds what at each time.

4

Generate & Copy Notes

Click "Generate Schedule" to create formatted text parts. Copy individual parts or all parts to paste into Kingshot's in-game chat!
